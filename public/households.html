Safety tips for transporting your hazardous waste

battery_std

Don’t mix or combine products.

delete

Keep products in their original containers whenever possible.

mode_edit

Label products that are not marked.

lock_outline

Secure products so they won’t tip or leak.

local_shipping

Cover and tie down your load. Please stay in your vehicle while unloading at the Auburn Wastemobile or traveling Wastemobile.

drive_eta

Don’t transport products in the passenger compartment of your vehicle.

If you live in King County, you can take your household hazardous waste to any of our four collection sites or to the traveling Wastemobile. You don’t need to make an appointment, and there’s no fee for this service.

What to bring

Limit 50 gallons per customer per day. No containers larger than 5 gallons. For larger quantities or unusual wastes, call the Household Hazards Line, Mon–Fri, 9:00am–4:30pm 206-296-4692 or toll free at 1-888-869-4233.
